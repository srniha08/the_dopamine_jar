# 🧪 The Dopamine Jar

Welcome to **The Dopamine Jar**! This is a simple, clean, and fun digital to-do list designed to turn your boring daily chores into a game. Every time you finish a task, you get a quick splash of colorful marble satisfaction.

---

## 🤔 What Does It Do?

* **Turn Chores into Serotonin:** Type in your tasks for the day and hit enter. 
* **Fun Checkbox Animation:** When you click a checkbox, the button itself flashes into a bright color, bounces, and sends a matching marble dropping down.
* **Realistic Marble Grid:** Marbles don't just float around randomly. They drop straight from the top of the glass jar, bounce a few times, and sit perfectly on the bottom floor, stacking neatly from left to right.
* **No Information Overload:** You can only add up to 15 tasks at a time. It keeps you focused without overwhelming your day!
* **Auto-Reset Every Day:** The app automatically saves your unfinished tasks but clears away yesterday's marbles when a new day starts so you can begin with a fresh slate.

---

## 🛠️ How It Was Built

This app runs incredibly fast because it doesn't use any heavy, complicated frameworks. It is built using pure, simple web code:

* **HTML5:** Creates the structural layout of the list and the glass jar container.
* **CSS3:** Handles the premium colors, smooth card shadows, and the custom gravity-bounce physics for the falling marbles.
* **JavaScript:** Calculates the strict grid coordinates so the marbles stack from the bottom up, tracks your task count, and handles saving everything.
* **Local Storage:** Saves your tasks directly inside your web browser's cache. No account or login required!

---

## 🚀 How to Run It on Your Computer

Running this project locally takes less than ten seconds:

1. **Download or Clone the Files:** Download this repository onto your machine.
2. **Open the Project:** Double-click the `index.html` file.
3. **That's It!** It will instantly open up and work perfectly inside any browser (Chrome, Edge, Safari, or Brave).
